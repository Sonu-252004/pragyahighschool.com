Welcome to PRAGYA +2 High School, Jamu

Established in 1993, PRAGYA +2 High School, Jamu, is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality education based on the New Education Policy 2020. Affiliated to both JAC (Jharkhand Academic Council) and CBSE up to the 10+2 level, our school follows the 10+2 pattern in English medium, fostering academic excellence and holistic development. Our state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated faculty, and vibrant community make us a beacon of learning in the region.

Academic Programmes

PRAGYA +2 High School follows the 10+2 curriculum affiliated to both JAC (Jharkhand Academic Council) and CBSE, in English medium, aligned with the New Education Policy 2020. Our academic programs are designed to promote critical thinking, creativity, and holistic development.

Science Stream

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and Computer Science for students seeking careers in engineering, medicine, and research.

Commerce Stream

Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and Informatics Practices for students pursuing business and finance.

Arts Stream

History, Geography, Political Science, and Sociology for students interested in humanities and social sciences.

Admissions

Admission to PRAGYA +2 High School is open for students seeking a quality education. Follow the steps below to apply.

Admission Procedure

  • Visit the school office or download the application form from the link below.
  • Submit the completed application form with the required documents (birth certificate, previous academic records, and passport-size photos).
  • Appear for an entrance examination and interview (if applicable).
  • Upon selection, complete the fee payment process as per the school guidelines.
